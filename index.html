What We Serve

Pigments for Every Industry

Our iron oxide pigments are used across a wide range of industries, delivering consistent color and performance.

Paint & Coatings

Vibrant, UV-resistant pigments for interior and exterior paints with excellent tinting strength.

Construction

Weather-stable pigments for colored concrete, interlocking bricks, and paving blocks.

Plastics & Rubber

Non-toxic, heat-resistant pigments for coloring plastic compounds and rubber products.

Ceramics

Inorganic pigments for ceramic glazes and body staining with excellent temperature stability.

Roofing Materials

Durable pigments for roof tiles and shingles that withstand extreme weather conditions.
