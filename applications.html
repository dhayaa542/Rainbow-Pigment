Industries We Serve

Iron Oxide Pigments for Every Application

Our high-quality iron oxide pigments are used across a diverse range of industries, delivering consistent performance and vibrant colour in demanding environments.

Industry Application

Paint & Coatings

Iron oxide pigments are the backbone of the paint and coatings industry. Our Yellow and Red Iron Oxide pigments provide exceptional tinting strength, UV resistance, and colour stability for both interior and exterior applications.

Whether used in decorative wall paints, industrial primers, anti-corrosion coatings, or automotive finishes, our pigments deliver consistent colour reproduction and long-lasting performance even under harsh environmental conditions.

✓ Interior wall paints
✓ Exterior coatings
✓ Industrial primers
✓ Anti-corrosion paints
Industry Application

Construction

In the construction industry, iron oxide pigments are used to colour concrete, cement, interlocking paving blocks, roofing tiles, and precast elements. Our pigments offer excellent weather stability, ensuring that coloured concrete structures maintain their appearance for years.

The alkali resistance of our pigments makes them particularly suitable for use in cementitious materials, where they can withstand the high pH environment without colour degradation.

✓ Coloured concrete
✓ Interlocking bricks
✓ Paving blocks
✓ Precast elements
Industry Application

Plastics & Rubber

Our iron oxide pigments are extensively used in the plastics and rubber industry for colouring PVC, polyethylene, polypropylene, and various rubber compounds. Being inorganic and non-toxic, they are safe for use in a wide range of applications.

The excellent heat stability of our pigments ensures consistent colour even during high-temperature processing, while their chemical inertness prevents interaction with polymer matrices.

✓ PVC compounds
✓ Rubber products
✓ Plastic masterbatch
✓ Moulded components
Industry Application

Ceramics

Iron oxide pigments are widely used in the ceramics industry for body staining and glaze colouration. Our pigments provide stable and consistent colours that withstand the high-temperature firing process used in ceramic production.

From floor and wall tiles to decorative pottery and sanitaryware, our pigments help manufacturers achieve the desired aesthetic appeal with reliable colour performance.

✓ Ceramic glazes
✓ Body staining
✓ Floor & wall tiles
✓ Decorative pottery
Industry Application

Roofing Materials

Roofing materials demand pigments that can withstand extreme weather conditions — intense sunlight, heavy rain, and temperature fluctuations. Our iron oxide pigments are engineered for exceptional durability in roofing applications.

Whether used in clay roof tiles, concrete roof tiles, or bituminous shingles, our pigments maintain their colour integrity and resist fading, ensuring roofs look great for years.

✓ Clay roof tiles
✓ Concrete tiles
✓ Bituminous shingles
✓ Metal roof coatings
