Our Story

Three Decades of Trusted Pigment Manufacturing

Founded in 1994 by late Mr.Niraipandiyan, Rainbow Pigment has grown from a local pigment supplier to one of Tamil Nadu's most trusted iron oxide pigment manufacturers. For over 31 years, we have been committed to delivering superior quality Yellow and Red Iron Oxide Pigments to industries across India.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide consistent, high-quality pigments that meet the demanding standards of the paint, construction, and industrial sectors. Today, we serve 500+ clients across 10+ states.

What sets us apart is not just our product quality — it's our deep understanding of our customers' needs, built over three decades of close partnerships with manufacturers, contractors, and industry professionals.
