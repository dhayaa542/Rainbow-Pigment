Yellow Oxide

Yellow Iron Oxide Pigments

Our Yellow Iron Oxide Pigments (Fe₂O₃·H₂O) are manufactured to deliver exceptional colour strength, consistency, and durability. Available in a range of shades from light lemon yellow to deep golden ochre, these pigments are ideal for applications demanding weather resistance and UV stability.

✓ UV Resistant
✓ Weather Stable
✓ Non-Toxic
✓ High Tinting Strength
✓ Chemical Inert

Applications

  • Paint & coatings (interior/exterior)
  • Concrete & paving blocks
  • Plastics & rubber compounds
  • Ceramic glazes
Red Oxide

Red Iron Oxide Pigments

Our Red Iron Oxide Pigments offer outstanding coverage, consistency, and lightfastness. Available in shades ranging from bright red to dark, these pigments are suitable for both industrial and decorative applications requiring long-lasting performance.

✓ UV Resistant
✓ Weather Stable
✓ Non-Toxic
✓ High Tinting Strength
✓ Heat Resistant

Applications

  • Paint & coatings (primers/finishes)
  • Construction – coloured concrete, bricks
  • Roofing tiles & shingles
  • Rubber & plastic compounds
  • Ceramic body staining
